The first ever magical tree

This tree was found in the forgotten forest of Alexia.

It was back-dated that who ever touches the tree recieves tramendous amount of power.

This was only a theory , no one actually dared to go there tho as rumors goes it can have a reverse effect on you if your heart is not pure.

Water benders claim to recieve mysterious power boost from the lake of Embyes

The lake Of Embyes was ruled by Prince Octavious , the name derives from his looks

No one was allowed at the lake , but things changed.Today its an Oasis for the people in the area.
